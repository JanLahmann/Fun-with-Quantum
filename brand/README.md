# Fun with Quantum — Brand Assets

The mark is **"the mid-roll die"** (design C2a-6): a die still tumbling, frame stroked in the
superposition gradient, one solid magenta pip (the *one* that measurement will deliver) over six
ghost cyan pips (the *six* still in superposition — on a real die, 1 and 6 are opposite faces and
can never be seen together; in superposition, they can). A motion arc marks the tumble.

> **Tagline tiers**
> - S: `GOD DOES PLAY DICE.` — merch, swag, social bios
> - M: `GOD DOES PLAY DICE. COME LEARN.` — default: footers, README banners, OG cards
> - L: `GOD DOES PLAY DICE. COME PLAY, BUILD, LEARN.` — hero, talk slides, press
> - DACH swap for the first sentence: `Gott würfelt doch.`

## Rules

- Use `favicon.svg` (the simplified cut) below ~24 px; the full mark's six ghost pips blur at
  favicon sizes.
- Ghost pips are what make the die ours — never render all pips solid.
- Wordmark face: Avenir Next / geometric sans, bold. Taglines: monospace, letterspaced, uppercase.
- The mark tilts −14° (favicon cut −12°). Don't straighten it: an upright die is a classical object.
